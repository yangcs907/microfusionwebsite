Microfusion Engineering Laboratories

Microfusion Engineering Laboratories, Inc (MEL) has been providing engineering services worldwide since 1995. We have extensive experiences in process controls, system integration, SCADA, telemetry, process network security and dynamic control simulation. MEL has serviced industries including power generations, utilities, manufacturing and pharmaceuticals.

Services

MEL provides automation migration projects on a full-scope turnkey basis or as an application services provider. The scope can include full design and implementation of the control system and instrumentation; construction of control/rack rooms; signal, power and network cable installation; and complete project management.

Digital Twins/Simulators

MEL offers the most cost-effective solutions in the simulation industry. MEL's Thermal Hydraulic Integrated Network (THINK™) models provide reliable simulators.

school Training

Combines real process scenarios and operator experience into simulations that improve training, reduce errors, and enhance system awareness.

factory Plant Operation

MEL provides THINK™ Process Digital Twins dynamic simulation software and services for an entire plant.

timeline Transient Analysis

Failures can trigger complex chain reactions and costly downtime. Simulation allows operators to train on realistic scenarios, improving response, minimizing damage, and safely testing alternative strategies without operational risk.

construction Staging

Commissioning delays and manual control checkouts can slow operations and increase costs. Simulation enables early testing of control logic, validates system behavior, and ensures controls are ready before plant startup.

notifications_active Alarm/HMI

Complex processes can trigger cascading alarms that overwhelm operators. Effective alarm management helps identify root causes quickly and guide corrective action. Simulation ensures strategies are tested, optimized, and safe before deployment.

verified NERC Validation

NERC CIP-007 mandates testing critical asset changes before deployment. MEL simulators provide production-like environments to validate changes and ensure compliance. NERC Security Guidelines for identifying Critical Cyber Assets can be reviewed by accessing the NERC website.

Controls

All areas and levels of control engineering needs are met through MEL's simulation quality assurance. Control projects are executed at the highest possible levels of quality.

sync_alt Automation Migration

Control systems improve efficiency, quality, and performance, but migrating legacy systems requires careful planning. MEL enables smooth, low-risk migrations using simulation-based validation, ensuring reliable implementation and project success.

engineering System Design

MEL is a team of control engineers and developers providing cost-effective solutions across DCS, PLC, SCADA, and system integration.

security SIL Systems

Reliable safety systems improve uptime, reduce costs, and ensure compliance. MEL designs and engineers SIL-rated BMS solutions that meet NFPA 85 & 86 standards.

dashboard HMI Optimization

Ineffective HMI design can obscure critical data and impact safety. MEL creates high-performance graphics that highlight key process information, improving operator response and system reliability. Simulation enables thorough validation before deployment.

notifications_active Alarm Management

Alarm overload can reduce operator effectiveness and compromise response time. Structured alarm strategies ensure alerts are relevant, actionable, and prioritized. MEL delivers optimized systems that support safe, efficient operations.

rocket_launch Virtual Startup

Virtual startup enables testing of control logic before plant startup. Simulation reduces risk, prevents costly damage, and ensures systems are fully validated prior to going online.

construction Commissioning

MEL's commissioning services are available to meet your critical system needs. Support is available for new applications as well as for re-commissioning services or "return to service" of equipment.
