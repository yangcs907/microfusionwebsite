Microfusion Engineering Laboratories

Microfusion Engineering Laboratories, Inc (MEL) has been providing engineering services worldwide since 1995. We have extensive experiences in process controls, system integration, SCADA, telemetry, process network security and dynamic control simulation. MEL has serviced industries including power generations, utilities, manufacturing and pharmaceuticals.

Services

MEL provides automation migration projects on a full-scope turnkey basis or as an application services provider. The scope can include full design and implementation of the control system and instrumentation; construction of control/rack rooms; signal, power and network cable installation; and complete project management.
